In this project, I'll be talking about anime. I'll be briefly discussing four different anime series here, and you can find the full details in the "Anime" menu above.

1- JJK

In the first season, The anime is about Itadori Yuji, a brilliant boy with a strong physique who hangs out with the school's Occult Club. One day, Yuji's life changes after the death of his grandfather and his encounter with Megumi Fushiguro, who tells him that he possesses a cursed object. From then on, trouble begins to unfold for Yuji....

In the second season, in a world where demons feed on humans who are beginning to lose themselves, the shards of the legendary and terrifying demon "Ryomen Sukuna" were scattered and lost. If any demon consumes parts of Sukuna's body, the power they gain could destroy the world as we know it. Fortunately, there is a mysterious school of sorcerers, the "Jujutsu" sorcerers, who live to protect this unstable universe from demons and the undead! Itadori Yuji is a high school student who spends his days visiting his bedridden grandfather. Although he seems like an ordinary teenager, his immense physical strength is something noteworthy! Every sports club wants him to join, but Itadori prefers to hang out with the outcasts in the Occult and Supernatural Club. One day, the club manages to get their hands on a sealed cursed object, but they have no idea of the terror they will unleash when they break the seal...

2-Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is an anime series set in Taisho-era Japan, where humans live in fear of demons who prey on them. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is brutally murdered by a demon, and his younger sister Nezuko is turned into one. Driven by a desire to avenge his family and find a cure for Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, an organization dedicated to exterminating demons. Along with his newfound friends, Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira, Tanjiro undergoes rigorous training to become a demon slayer. Together, they embark on perilous missions to defeat powerful demons while unraveling the dark secrets behind their existence.

3-Death Note

Death Note is a popular Japanese anime and manga series that centers around a high school student named Light Yagami, who discovers a mysterious notebook with supernatural powers. This "Death Note" allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it, as long as he visualizes their face. Light, initially disgusted by the criminal activities in the world, decides to use the Death Note to create a crime-free society by eliminating criminals. He takes on the alias "Kira" and becomes a vigilante figure, striking fear into the hearts of criminals worldwide. However, as Light's killings increase, he attracts the attention of a brilliant detective known as "L," who is determined to unmask Kira and bring him to justice. A thrilling cat-and-mouse game ensues between the two, with both characters displaying exceptional intelligence and strategic thinking.

4-Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, an orphaned boy living in the Hidden Leaf Village, who dreams of becoming Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of his village. Naruto is feared and ostracized because he carries the Nine-Tailed Fox demon, a powerful creature sealed inside him when he was a baby. Despite the hardship, Naruto remains optimistic and works hard to gain the respect of the villagers. Throughout his journey, Naruto forms strong bonds with his teammates: Sasuke Uchiha, a talented but vengeful ninja, and Sakura Haruno. Together, under the mentorship of Kakashi Hatake, they go on missions and face enemies, including rogue ninjas and the Akatsuki organization, which hunts down the tailed beasts like the one inside Naruto.
